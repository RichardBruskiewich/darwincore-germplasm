-----------------
PURL.og/germplasm
-----------------

The germplasm domain of the purl.org was established in March 2012 to provide persistant
URIs for germplasm terminology.

The Germplasm Vocabulary was established in August 2009 to provide an extension to the 
TDWG Darwin Core standard for publishing gene bank data sets on plant genetic resources. 
The Germplasm Vocabulary was upgraded in August 2012 with a SKOS/RDF serialisation.

Contact: Dag Endresen [dag.endresen@gmail.com] for further information.

About PURL:
-----------

The OCLC PURL Service has been running since the beginning of January, 1996.
PURLs were developed and is maintained by the OCLC Online Computer Library Center, Inc. 
OCLC is a nonprofit computer library service and research organization whose computer 
network and services link more than 21,000 libraries in 63 countries and territories.

The PURLs are resolved by the http://purl.org/ as a HTTP redirection service. A PURL has 
three parts: (1) a protocol, (2) a resolver address, and (3) a name.

     http://purl.org/germplasm/germplasmTerm#TERM
     ----   -------- --------------------
       /        |               \
  protocol  resolver address     name
